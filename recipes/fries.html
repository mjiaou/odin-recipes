Quick and Easy Home Fries

Sliced home fries with seasoning.

Quick and Easy Home Fries make for a crispy and savory breakfast. This is a quick and easy breakfast recipe for you or your family. The recipe only requires potatoes, butter, and seasoning. Quick and Easy Home Fries are great as a breakfast or even a side dish. Try it for dinner too!

Ingredients

  • 3 medium russet potatoes, cubed
  • 3 tablespoons butter or margarine
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

    • Steps

    1. Rinse potato cubes with cold water and drain well.
    2. Place potatoes in the skillet and season with salt and pepper; mix well.
    3. Cover and cook for 10 minutes.
    4. Remove the lid and cook, turning frequently, until brown and crisp on all sides, about 10 more minutes.
